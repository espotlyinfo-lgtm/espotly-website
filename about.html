The Story

Parking shouldn't be a reason not to go.

If you've ever driven around Makati, BGC, Ortigas, or Quezon City, you already know the feeling. You have somewhere to be — a dinner, a meeting, a quick errand — and before you even get there, parking becomes the thing you're already dreading.

For eSpotly founder Esmie Canoog, this wasn't just an occasional inconvenience. It was a recurring reality. The circling, the full lots, the last-minute stress — it got to a point where parking alone was enough to make her not want to go out at all.

But she also noticed something else: private spaces sitting completely empty — in condos, buildings, and homes — while drivers went around the block for the third time looking for anywhere to park.

The problem wasn't a shortage of parking. It was a disconnect.

Esmie Canoog

Founder & CEO, eSpotly

"Parking was always the reason I'd almost cancel plans. I knew I wasn't the only one feeling this way — and I knew there had to be a better way."
Our Mission

Connect the spot to the driver.

eSpotly is a parking marketplace that connects drivers who need a reliable space with owners who have one to offer. Every booking is confirmed, every payment is secure, and every space is verified before it goes live.

We believe city life in the Philippines shouldn't be held back by parking. Whether you're heading to work, meeting friends, or running errands — finding a spot should be the last thing on your mind.
