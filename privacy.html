01 Introduction

eSpotly is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your data.

02 Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email, mobile number, profile photo
  • Identity Verification (Owners only): Government-issued ID and proof of ownership
  • Payment Information: PayMongo processes payments; eSpotly does not store full card numbers
  • Usage Data: Booking history, listing activity, messages, reviews, interactions
  • Location Data: General location only; no background tracking
  • Device Information: Device type, OS, and app version
  • SMS OTP Data: Mobile number and one-time passwords for account verification

03 How We Use Your Information

  • Account management, bookings, and payments
  • Owner identity verification
  • Booking, support, and update communications
  • Notifications you opted into
  • SMS OTP verification for secure account creation
  • Service improvement and new features
  • Fraud detection and legal compliance

04 Information Sharing

  • Other users: Name and photo for confirmed bookings only
  • Sharetribe (transaction management)
  • PayMongo (payment processing)
  • Semaphore (SMS OTP delivery)
  • Cloudinary (image hosting)
  • Law enforcement when required by law

05 Data Security

  • All data transmitted with HTTPS/SSL encryption
  • Passwords hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Industry-standard security measures
  • Regular security reviews
  • No absolute security guarantee

06 Data Retention

  • Data retained while account is active and as required by law
  • OTP codes deleted after verification or 10 minutes, whichever is first

07 Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your data → support@espotly.com
  • Request data portability
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

08 Children

eSpotly is not for users under 18 years old.

09 Changes to This Policy

We will notify you via app or email for significant changes.
