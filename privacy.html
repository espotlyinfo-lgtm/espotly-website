01 Introduction

eSpotly is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your data when you use the eSpotly platform.

02 Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, mobile number, and profile photo
  • Identity Verification (Owners only): Government-issued ID and proof of ownership
  • Payment Information: PayMongo processes payments; eSpotly does not store full card numbers
  • Usage Data: Booking history, listing activity, messages, reviews, and interactions
  • Location Data: General location only; no background tracking
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • SMS OTP Data: Mobile number and one-time passwords for account verification

03 How We Use Your Information

  • Account management, bookings, and payment processing
  • Owner identity verification
  • Booking confirmations, support messages, and service updates
  • Notifications you have opted into
  • SMS OTP verification for secure account creation
  • Service improvement and development of new features
  • Fraud detection and legal compliance

04 Information Sharing

We share your information with:

  • Other users: Name and photo are shared for confirmed bookings only
  • Third-party service providers — all under confidentiality agreements:
    • Sharetribe — transaction management
    • PayMongo — payment processing
    • Semaphore — SMS OTP delivery
    • Cloudinary — image hosting
  • Law enforcement when required by applicable law

05 Data Security

  • All data transmitted with HTTPS/SSL encryption
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Industry-standard security measures applied throughout
  • Regular security reviews conducted
  • No absolute security guarantee can be made for any online system

06 Data Retention

  • Data is retained while your account is active and as required by applicable law
  • OTP codes are deleted after successful verification or 10 minutes, whichever occurs first

07 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data — contact us at support@espotly.com
  • Request data portability in a commonly used format
  • Opt out of non-essential communications at any time

08 Children

eSpotly is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

09 Changes to This Policy

We will notify you via the app or email of any significant changes to this Privacy Policy. Your continued use of eSpotly after such notice constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
