Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using eSpotly

How to Use the App

How do I book a parking space?
Browse spaces Select space Tap "Book Now" Pay via GCash / GrabPay / Maya / Card Wait for owner approval Get access details
Can I cancel a booking?

Yes. Check the cancellation policy before booking. Tap "Cancel Booking" in the app. Refunds follow the policy set at booking time.

How do I contact the owner?

Use in-app messaging on your booking screen.

What if the owner doesn't give me access?

Message the owner first through in-app messaging. If there's no response, contact support@espotly.com with your booking details and we'll assist you.

Payment Questions

Can I pay the owner directly outside the app?

No — external transactions are not covered by eSpotly protection.

Is it safe to pay through the app?

Yes — payments are processed by PayMongo with bank-level encryption. Your card details are never stored.

What payment methods are available?
GCash GrabPay Maya Credit / Debit Card
What if payment fails?

Your booking will be automatically cancelled after 2 hours. No charges will be made.

Safety Tips

How do I stay safe when using eSpotly?
  • Try to arrive and inspect the space during daylight hours.
  • Check the owner's reviews and ratings before booking.
  • Take photos of the space before and after your booking as a personal record.
  • Report any damage or issues to support@espotly.com with photos and your booking details.
  • Never share personal information with the owner outside the app.
Is my vehicle insured while parked?

eSpotly does not provide vehicle insurance. We recommend verifying that your own comprehensive insurance covers parked vehicles.

How Your Data Is Protected

Who can see my personal information?

Only you and the owner of a confirmed booking can see your personal info.

Is my payment information secure?

Payments are processed by PayMongo with bank-level encryption — card numbers are never stored.

How is my data stored and for how long?
  • Photos and ID documents are stored securely on Cloudinary.
  • Your data is retained while your account is active.
  • Location: general only — no background tracking.
  • Email support@espotly.com to access or delete your data.

When do I get my payout?

Automatically at 9:00 AM PHT the day after the booking end date.

It covers payment processing, app operations, customer support, SMS verification, and server costs.

What if a driver's payment fails?

The booking is automatically cancelled after 2 hours. Your space becomes available again immediately.
