01 Acceptance of Terms

By using eSpotly, you agree to these Terms & Conditions. If you do not agree, do not use the app.

02 Service Description

eSpotly is a platform connecting parking owners with drivers in the Philippines. eSpotly is not a party to booking agreements between users.

03 How the App Works

  • Owner lists space
  • Driver books
  • Owner accepts or declines
  • Driver pays via PayMongo
  • Owner grants access
  • Both parties leave reviews after the booking ends

04 User Accounts

Accurate information is required. Owners must complete identity verification before accepting bookings.

05 Owner Obligations

The listed space must be safe, legal, and accurately described. Owners must respond to booking requests and grant access as agreed.

06 Driver Obligations

Use the space only as booked. Respect the property and owner's rules. Drivers are responsible for any damage caused during the booking period.

07 Booking Rules

  • A booking is confirmed only after payment is received and the owner accepts
  • All bookings and transactions must go through the eSpotly app only — external transactions are not covered by eSpotly protection

08 Payments & Fees

  • All payments processed via PayMongo (GCash, GrabPay, Maya, Card)
  • eSpotly charges a 15% platform fee, deducted before owner payout
  • Owner payouts are processed automatically at 9:00 AM PHT the day after the booking ends

09 Cancellation & Refunds

  • Cancellation policies vary by listing and are set by the owner
  • Bookings are automatically cancelled if the driver does not pay within 2 hours of owner acceptance
  • Refunds are available the same day only, before 00:01 midnight, for valid reasons: owner no-show, spot unavailable, spot mismatch, double-booking, or system error
  • Refund requests submitted after midnight are non-refundable

10 User-Generated Content (UGC) Liability

Users are solely responsible for their own content. eSpotly reserves the right to remove content that violates laws, contains hate speech, is fraudulent or harmful, infringes copyrights, or otherwise violates these Terms.

11 Chat & Messaging

eSpotly provides in-app messaging between drivers and owners. Chat messages and support data are stored securely for operational and dispute resolution purposes. Do not share personal payment information, passwords, or contact details outside the app.

12 Dispute Resolution & Arbitration

Any disputes between eSpotly, drivers, and owners shall be resolved through binding arbitration under Philippine Arbitration Law, rather than court proceedings. Both parties agree to submit to arbitration as the sole remedy for disputes. eSpotly will mediate in good faith; final decisions are binding.

For disputes, contact support@espotly.com.

16 Privacy & Data Protection

User data is stored securely in compliance with the Philippine Data Privacy Act (PDPA). Core user data (profile, booking info) is managed by Sharetribe. Chat messages and support data are stored securely for operational purposes.

Users have the right to access, correct, or delete their personal data. Contact support@espotly.com for data requests. eSpotly will respond within 15 business days of verified requests.
