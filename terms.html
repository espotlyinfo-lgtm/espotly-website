02 Service Description

eSpotly connects parking space owners with drivers seeking parking in the Philippines. eSpotly acts as a platform intermediary — not a party to any booking agreement between owner and driver.

03 How the App Works

  • Owners list available parking spaces with details, rates, and availability
  • Drivers browse, search, and book available spaces
  • Owner accepts or declines booking request
  • Driver completes payment via PayMongo
  • Booking confirmed once payment received
  • Owner provides access; driver uses space during booked period
  • After booking ends, both parties can leave reviews

04 User Accounts

Must provide accurate info. Responsible for account security and all activities under your account. Owners must complete identity verification before accepting bookings.

05 Owner Obligations

  • Spaces must be available, safe, accessible, and legally available as described
  • Must comply with local regulations, HOA rules, and property laws
  • Maintain space cleanliness and safety
  • Provide accurate availability and pricing
  • Respond to driver inquiries in reasonable time
  • Grant access as agreed

06 Driver Obligations

  • Use spaces only for the booked period and stated purpose
  • Respect the property and the owner's rules
  • Report damages or issues immediately
  • Responsible for any damage caused during booking
  • Do not sublicense or share the space with others
  • Park vehicle safely and legally within the space

07 Booking Rules

  • All bookings are subject to owner availability and confirmation
  • Owner has the right to accept or decline any booking request
  • A booking is confirmed only after payment is received and the owner accepts
  • A driver cannot book a space already booked by another driver
  • False or misleading booking information may result in cancellation and account suspension
  • All bookings and payments must occur through the eSpotly app. External transactions are not covered by eSpotly protection.

08 Payments and Fees

  • All payments are processed via PayMongo (supports GCash, GrabPay, Maya, and Card)
  • eSpotly charges a 15% platform fee, deducted before owner payout
  • Payments are non-refundable except as outlined in the Cancellation Policy
  • PayMongo handles secure payment processing; eSpotly does not store card details
  • Owner payouts are processed automatically at 9:00 AM PHT the day after the end date of the booking

09 Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellation policies vary by listing and are set by the owner
  • Refunds are subject to the cancellation policy displayed at the time of booking
  • Drivers must cancel through the app
  • Owners may cancel with a valid reason (e.g. emergency, accessibility issue)
  • If a driver fails to pay within 2 hours of booking acceptance, the booking is automatically cancelled
  • eSpotly may mediate disputes regarding cancellations

10 Dispute Resolution

In case of disputes between a driver and an owner, contact eSpotly support at support@espotly.com.

  • eSpotly will review evidence and attempt mediation
  • eSpotly's decision is final and binding
  • For unresolved disputes, either party may escalate to local authorities or small claims court
